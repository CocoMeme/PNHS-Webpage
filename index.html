About

Pateros National High School

Pateros National High School is a premier educational institution dedicated to nurturing students in both academic and personal excellence. We pride ourselves on fostering a supportive and dynamic learning environment.

  • Committed to providing quality education for all students.
  • Equipping learners with skills for lifelong success.
  • Promoting values of respect, leadership, and integrity.

At Pateros National High School, we believe every student has the potential to achieve greatness. Through innovative teaching methods, diverse programs, and a strong sense of community, we empower learners to excel in every aspect of their journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about Pateros National High School

Enrollment at PNHS is straightforward. Visit our school’s registrar office during the enrollment period with the required documents, including your previous school records, birth certificate, and ID photos. For online registration, check the school’s official website or Facebook page for guidelines.

PNHS offers a wide range of programs, including the Basic Education Curriculum, Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), and Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strands. We also have special programs for the arts, sports, and science.

PNHS provides a holistic learning experience through its skilled faculty, modern facilities, and extracurricular activities. Our guidance office supports students’ emotional and career needs, while various clubs and organizations help develop leadership and social skills. Additionally, we host seminars and competitions to enhance students' potential.
